# Revenue Performance Dashboard - Portfolio Case Study

## Project Goal

Create an executive-ready Power BI dashboard that helps a revenue team monitor sales performance, customer retention, opportunity conversion, and individual sales representative contribution.

## Business Questions Answered

- How much revenue is the business generating, and how is it trending?
- Are revenue, order volume, and average order value improving month over month?
- Which sales representatives are driving the most revenue and pipeline?
- Where are opportunities converting or getting stuck in the funnel?
- Which customer segments and regions contribute most to retention and growth?

## Dashboard Experience

- `Executive Overview`: revenue KPIs, conversion metrics, target achievement, and revenue trend.
- `Customer Retention`: active customer and retention performance by month, segment, and region.
- `Sales Funnel`: opportunity pipeline, won/lost performance, stage analysis, and region filters.
- `Sales Rep Performance`: rep-level ranking, contribution share, pipeline value, win rate, and time intelligence.

## Technical Highlights

- Built as a Power BI Project (`.pbip`) so the report and semantic model are version-control friendly.
- Added advanced DAX measures for MTD, QTD, YTD, MoM, QoQ, YoY, rank, contribution share, and rep scoring.
- Organized measures in `Revenue_measures` for easier review and maintenance.
- Added professional light/dark JSON themes for polished presentation.
- Preserved source-table relationships across customers, products, sales reps, orders, opportunities, and activities.
